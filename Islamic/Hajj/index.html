HAJJ

  • Isalm ka paanchwa rukun Hajj hai aur jiske paas itne paise hon ke Hajj kar sake is par Hajj karna farz hai,
  • Makka mauzana jaakar Arfaat mein jama hona aur iske sab arkaan adaa karne ko Hajj kehte hain,
  • aur Allah Ta'ala bhi kehte hain ke jis ne Hajj karliya mai iske tamaam umar ke gunah mu'af kardeta hoon,
  • bachcho tumhe maloom hai ke Makka mauzama mein Khana-e-Kaaba hai jis taraf hum moo kar ke namaz padhte hain isko Baitullah yani Allah ka ghar kehte hain,
  • Allah Ta'ala ke hukm se ye Hazrat. Ibrahim ne banaya tha,
  • haji aur musalmaan raat din iska tawaf karte rehte hain yani iske gird chakar lagate hue Allah Ta'ala ki tareef karte rehte hain aur duaein maangte rehte hain, is tarah jis tarah ek parwana roshni ke gird ghoomta rehta hai, is tarah Allah Ta'ala ke aashiq is ghar ke gird ghoomte hain iski tareef bayan karte rehte hain.
Bachcho: Jab tum bade hojao aur balig hojao to is farz ko zaroor adaa karna hamare pyare Nabi ne farmaya iska matlab ye hai ke jis par Hajj farz hua aur isne na kiya to wo yahud hokar mare ya nasrani hokar. Tauba tauba. Allah Ta'ala hum sabko musalmaan hokar maut dein, Ameen. Ab chand aayatein Hajj ke mutaliq hum Qur'an Majeed mein se naql karte hain. وَإِذْ بَوَّأْنَا لِإِبْرَاهِيمَ مَكَانَ الْبَيْتِ أَنْ لَا تُشْرِكْ بِي شَيْئًا وَطَهِرْ بَيْتِيَ لِلطَّائِفِينَ وَالقَابِمِينَ وَالرُّكَّعِ السُّجُودِ وَأَذِنُ فِي النَّاسِ بِالْحَجِ يَأْتُوكَ رِجَالًا وَعَلَى كُلِّ ضَامِرٍ يَأْتِينَ مِنْ كُلِّ فَجٍّ عَمِيون Aur jab ke humne Ibrahim ko Khana-e-Kaaba ki jagah batladi aur hukm diya ke mere saath kisi cheez ko shareek mat karna aur mere is ghar ko tawaf karne walon ke aur namaz mein qiyam ruku wa sajda karne walon ke liye paak rakhna aur Ibrahim se ye bhi kaha gaya ke logon mein Hajj farz hone ka ailaan kardo log tumhare paas Hajj ko chale aayenge piyada bhi aur dubli oontniyon par bhi jo ke door daraaz raaston se pahunchi hongi. [Surah Hajj, 26-27]

Haan bachcho, Hajj Islam ka ek bahut hi ahem rukun hai aur iski bahut fazeelat hai. Ab hum dekhte hain ke tumne iske baare mein kya samjha. Ye rahe kuch sawalaat:
* Islam ka paanchwa rukun kaun sa hai?
* Hajj kis par farz hai?
* Hajj ke arkaan adaa karne ke liye musalmaanon ko kahaan jama hona hota hai?
* Hajj kis mauqe par hota hai?
* Arfaat ke maidaan mein jama hokar log kya karte hain?
* Hajj karne wale ke liye Allah Ta'ala ne kya fazeelat bayan ki hai?
* Makka mauzama mein us muqaddas jagah ka kya naam hai jis taraf musalmaan namaz mein moo karte hain?
* Khana-e-Kaaba kisne banaya tha?
* Haji aur deegar musalmaan Khana-e-Kaaba ke gird kya karte hain? Is amal ko kya kehte hain?
* Hamare pyare Nabi ﷺ ne us shakhs ke baare mein kya farmaya jis par Hajj farz ho aur woh na kare?
* Qur'an Majeed ki kis Surah ki aayat Hajj ke farz hone ka zikr karti hai?
* Surah Hajj ki in aayaton mein Allah Ta'ala ne Hazrat Ibrahim (alaihis salam) ko Khana-e-Kaaba ke baare mein kya hukm diye?
* Aayat ke mutabiq, Hajj ke liye log kis tarah Makka pahunchte hain?
